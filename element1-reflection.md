# Element 1 – Reflection

**Module:** Artificial Intelligence  
**University:** University of Bradford  
**Date Submitted:** February 2026  

---

## What was this activity?

Element 1 was a formal assessed piece of coursework for my AI module. It required me to research and write about key topics in artificial intelligence that we had been studying in lectures.

## What did I do?

I read through the assignment brief carefully and planned what I needed to cover. I used the lecture slides and some online resources to research the topics. I wrote a first draft, reviewed it, and submitted it on time through Canvas before the deadline.

## What did I learn?

This assignment helped me understand AI concepts better by having to explain them in writing rather than just reading about them. I found it challenging to put technical ideas into my own words, but working through it helped things make more sense to me. I also improved my academic writing and referencing skills.

## What would I do differently next time?

When I received my tutor feedback, I noted that I could have used more specific examples to support my points. Next time I will try to include more real-world examples of AI being used, rather than keeping my answers too general. I will also start planning earlier so I have more time to revise my draft.

## Relevance to my career

Being able to explain AI concepts clearly in writing is an important skill for any job in technology. Whether I am writing a report, an email to a client, or documentation for a project, I need to communicate technical ideas in a way that makes sense. This assignment was good practice for that.
