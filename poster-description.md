# Exhibition Poster – Description and Reflection

**Project:** Conversational AI Agent for Citizen Support  
**Event:** University of Bradford Project Exhibition  
**Date:** April 2026  

---

## About the poster

I designed and produced the exhibition poster for our group AI project. The poster was displayed at the university exhibition where members of the public, staff and other students could view our work.

The poster was created using Canva and printed at A1 size.

---

## What the poster covered

The poster explained our group project to a general audience. It included:

- What the project is: a conversational AI agent that helps citizens find information about local council services
- Why it matters: many people find council websites confusing — a chatbot can answer questions in plain English
- How it works: a simple diagram showing how a user asks a question and the agent finds and returns an answer
- What we used: the technologies and tools the team used to build the agent
- My contribution: the dataset I created and the research I carried out

---

## What I learnt from making the poster

Designing the poster taught me how to communicate a technical project to people who are not technical. I had to think about what a member of the public would actually want to know, rather than just writing about the technical details that interested me as a student.

I made two drafts. My first draft was too text-heavy. After feedback from my team leader, I simplified the wording and used more visual layout to make it easier to read at a glance.
